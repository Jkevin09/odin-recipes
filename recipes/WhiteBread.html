White Bread of the Bread Machine

ingredients

  • 1 cup warm water (110 degrees F/45 degrees C)
  • 3 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 3 tablepoons white sugar
  • 1 ½ teaspoons salt
  • 3 cups bread flour
  • 2 ¼ teaspoons active dry yeast

Directions

Step 1

Gather all ingredients.

Step 2

Place water, sugar, oil, salt, bread flour, and yeast into the pan of the bread
machine (or in the order recommended by your bread machine manufacture).

Step 3

Bake on White Bread setting,

Step 4

Use oven mitts to carefully remove the bread an from the machine.

Step 5

Remove bread form the pan and let cool completely on a wire reack brefore slicing.
